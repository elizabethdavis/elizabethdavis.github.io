    My Journey at WEX: 5+ Years of Growth and Innovation in Product Design

    Introduction

    Since joining WEX in 2019, my career in product design has been defined by growth, innovation, and impactful contributions in benefits software. I’ve had the privilege of shaping digital experiences that impact over 20 million benefit account users. This journey has been more than a collection of projects—it’s been a continual evolution, marked by key achievements and valuable lessons learned along the way.


    Career Progression

    2019: Embarking on the WEX Journey

    I started at WEX as an Individual Contributor, eager to apply my UX skills to the complex world of benefits software. From day one, I was immersed in challenging projects that pushed me to grow both creatively and technically. I was responsible for the redesign of 1 portal and started working on feature updates in another portal.

    2020: Rising to Leadership

    After just a year and a half, my dedication and innovative approach to UX design were recognized with a promotion to a leadership position. This new role allowed me to not only continue hands-on design work but also to guide and inspire my small but mighty team of 2 as we managed design work across 3 platforms on web and mobile applications.

    2022: Growing My Team

    After increased demand for UX design partnership across the organization, our team has continued to grow. Through a company acquisition of Benefit Administration software and a design team reorg, we restructured our design team to help manage the workload. As a result, my team grew to 4 direct reports within Benefits and grew to manage 5 benefit portals.

    Today: Leading a Dynamic Design Team and Benefits Design Strategy

    Presently, I manage a team of 5 talented UX professionals. Together, we tackle 20-25 efforts annually ranging from large scale redesigns to smaller efforts, driving innovation and user-centric design in benefits software solutions. Our work impacts millions of users, simplifying complex processes and enhancing the overall experience of managing benefits across 5 portals on web and mobile platforms.

    Personal Growth and Lessons Learned

    My years at WEX have been transformative, both professionally and personally. Here are some key lessons and areas of growth:

    Balancing Vision and Execution

    Learning to think strategically while still being able to roll up my sleeves and contribute to hands-on design work.

    The Power of Empathy

    Deepening my understanding of user needs and how to translate those into effective design solutions through the Product Operating Model.

    Leadership Through Trust

    Developing a leadership style that empowers team members to take ownership and innovate.

    Navigating Complexity

    Honing my ability to simplify complex systems and processes for end-users without losing necessary functionality.

    Data-Driven Design

    Strengthening my skills in using data and analytics to inform design decisions and measure impact.

    Continuous Improvement

    Embracing the mindset of lifelong learning, consistently seeking out new tools, techniques, and feedback to enhance both my skills and the design process.
