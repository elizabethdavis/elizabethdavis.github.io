Hi! I'm Elizabeth. I'm a User Experience Designer based in NYC but I have a passion for painting, drawing, hand lettering, and creating. Because of this, I started photographing my work and posting them on my instagram account @edavisartwork.

I grew up on the Jersey Shore and take a lot of inspiration from the endless days I spent on the beach every summer. I love coloring my work with many different shades of blue and green, like the colors of the ocean that have influenced my life in so many positive ways. I love making pieces for people who connect with the ocean as much as I do, and I love lettering quotes that mean so much to them!

On this site you'll find some projects that I have worked on, some features from Instagram, and a contact form where you can reach out! I would love to hear from you and would be happy to customize a special piece that you can bring into your home.

Allison's Baby Shower

March 2018

For this project, I was asked to make four different 4" x 6" cards with requested quotes. I was given a color theme to follow (teal blue and gray), but had a lot of creative freedom for how to design the quotes. After the party, the quotes were put into frames and now hang in the baby's nursery.



Katie and David's Wedding

August 2017

For this project, I was asked to letter ~20 different quotes that would become a part of the centerpieces on each table at the wedding reception. The color theme was purple, gold, and black, and all of the quotes were advice or past quotes about love and marriage from friends and family of the bride and groom.
