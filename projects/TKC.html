What is The Kindness Counts?

The Kindness Counts is all about things that count. Between unrealistic instagram profiles to hearing horrible stories on the news, we need to find a space to be real, and kind - to ourselves, our environment, and to the people around us. This is a creative, safe place where my goal is to make myself better as an environmental advocate, a friend, a resource, and most importantly, as a person.

Here you will find collections of resources that help you be kind to the planet, artwork that may inspire you to spread the message of kindness, music to help brighten your day, and so much more. My intention and driving force is to bring value to my life and yours in a meaningful, honest way. To me, that means sharing my knowledge and learnings in a format that benefits your life, not taking from it. So stick around, explore, and follow me on my journey to Make Today Count.

About Me

Hi there! My name is Elizabeth Davis, the creator of The Kindness Counts. I wanted to start something that makes and impactful difference, because sitting around watching the world fall apart was making me miserable. In looking for resources on how to make a difference, I realized that it starts with just one person. However, there is a ton of information out there and it can get overwhelming quickly.

I wanted to create a safe space that could act as a resource for finding products that make a difference, how you can help in your everyday life, and encourage people to become the best version of themselves. If any of these things sounds like its up your alley, take a look at my videos, resources, or sign up for my newsletter that recaps everything I talk about every month. I hope you enjoy, and make today count!

Elizabeth Davis
