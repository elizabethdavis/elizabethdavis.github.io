    • About Me

    Great design leadership harmonizes bold ideas with user-centric solutions.

    Hello! I'm a passionate UX leader with over 8 years of experience in crafting exceptional digital experiences. As a Senior UX Manager, I blend strategic thinking with user-centric design to create products that delight users and drive business success. My dual background in Computer Science and Interactive Design uniquely positions me to bridge the gap between design and development teams.

    I thrive in collaborative, cross-functional teams and excel at balancing meticulous detail with big-picture thinking to enhance user satisfaction and engagement. Whether I'm facilitating design sprints, conducting user research, or aligning UX strategy with business goals, I'm always focused on delivering solutions that have real impact. My expertise spans from wireframing and prototyping to design systems and usability testing, all underpinned by a deep commitment to understanding user needs and motivations.
