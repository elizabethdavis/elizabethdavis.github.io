About Me

Hello! My name is Elizabeth Davis and I am a user experience designer currently based in NYC. I have two degrees from The College of New Jersey (TCNJ) – a BS in Computer Science and a BA in Interactive Multimedia – and my job in design is the perfect combination of both.
I love to solve complex problems and use my imagination to make the world around me better. I have a little over three years of experience in the field and have been loving every minute of it. My greatest strengths include learning new techniques quickly, adapting on-the-go in an agile environment, and bringing my own set of fresh ideas. Let’s change the world!
