About Me

Hello! My name is Elizabeth Davis and I am a user experience designer currently based in NYC. I have two degrees from The College of New Jersey (TCNJ) – a BS in Computer Science and a BA in Interactive Multimedia – and my job is the perfect combination of both. As a designer with both a technical and creative background, I am uniquely positioned to effectively communicate my designs between designers and development teams. I know that diving into pixel perfect designs are just as important as analyzing the big picture, and I am always looking for an opportunity to learn something new.

I love to solve complex problems and use my imagination and creativity to make the world around me better. I have a bright, creative mind with a passion for learning and innovation. I love to take inspiration from being outside, whether it is at the beach or on a hike. I have a little over three years of experience in the field and have been loving every minute of it. My greatest strengths include learning new techniques quickly, adapting on-the-go in an agile environment, and bringing my own set of fresh ideas. Let’s change the world!
